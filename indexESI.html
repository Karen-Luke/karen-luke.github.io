Welcome to Equestrian Solutions International

Equestrian Solutions International is a consultancy that focuses on helping both equestrian organisations and horse owners.

Founder, Karen Luke has extensive experience riding, coaching, and training horses and enjoys competing in eventing. She is currently completing a PhD where she is researching horse welfare, rider safety and rider satisfaction, and is the author of several peer-reviewed publications.
